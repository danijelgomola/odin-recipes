Serbian Salad

Description

It's nice, fresh, summery tomato salad with a kick! It's wonderful and easy-to-make salad.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup red tomatoes wedges
  • ½ cup cubed cucumber
  • ¼ cup diced red onion
  • ¼ cup extra-virgin olive oil
  • salt to taste
  • 1 banana pepper, sliced into ¼-inch rings
  • parsley for garnish

Steps

  1. Combine tomatoes, cucumber, and red onion in a bowl.
  2. Drizzle with olive oil; stir.
  3. Season with salt.
  4. Sprinkle banana pepper slices on top; garnish with parsley.
